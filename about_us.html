Who We Are

Travela is a leading flight booking platform established in 2015 with a passion for connecting people to their dream destinations. Based in San Francisco, we’ve grown from a small startup to a global travel service, helping millions of travelers book flights with ease and confidence.

Our platform partners with over 300 airlines worldwide, offering unbeatable prices, flexible booking options, and 24/7 customer support. Whether you're planning a spontaneous getaway or a business trip, we’re here to simplify your journey.

At Travela, we believe travel should be accessible to everyone. That’s why we’ve built cutting-edge technology to provide real-time flight updates, personalized recommendations, and secure payment systems—all in one place.
